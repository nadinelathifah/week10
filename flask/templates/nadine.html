about me

Hi, my name is Nadine!
And here is a little glimpse into my life ★

I am originally from Jakarta, the capital city of Indonesia. I spent the first 4 years between Brunei and Indonesia, and the subsequent 14 years in Qatar. Once I completed secondary school, I moved to the UK to pursue higher studies, where I ended up finding a passion for coding!

Something that I enjoy is going cafe-hopping with friends. There's always new places to go and new food combos and drinks to try. I like going on walks (but not too far lol). I also enjoy playing table tennis.

Since graduating, I've taken time to figure out where my passions lie and where I see myself excelling at in the future. Many possibilities await. But for now, it's a working progress...
